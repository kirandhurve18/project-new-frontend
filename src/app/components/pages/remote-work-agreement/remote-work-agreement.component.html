Remote Work Agreement

Effective Date: 01/01/2026

BACKGROUND
  1. The purpose of this Policy is to outline the policy guidelines and eligibility requirements regarding Remote Work applicable for all the Employees of the Destek Infosolutions Pvt. Ltd.
  2. Remote Work allows Employees to work in their own home or in a satellite location for all their work assigned by the Company.
  3. This Policy is applicable for both the temporary and permanent Remote Work assigned to any Employees of the Company.
  4. All Employees are responsible for understanding and adhering to this Policy.
ELIGIBILITY
  1. Before entering into any Remote Work Agreement, the Employee and manager/supervisor will evaluate the suitability.
  2. The Employee and the Company will discuss the job responsibilities and determine if the job is appropriate.
  3. The Company will approve the Remote Work on an ad hoc or regular basis as suitable.
  4. Such approval will be reviewed in terms of cost, security, safety, communication, and reporting.
  5. Remote Work will be subject to continuing approval of the Company.
COMPENSATION AND BENEFITS
  1. The compensation of the Employee while working remotely will be as per the terms of the Employment Agreement.
SCHEDULE
  1. Employees must ensure availability over telephone, email and other messenger applications during working hours.
  2. Employees are required to record all hours worked in the Timesheets on the Employee Portal by 11:00 pm daily.
EMPLOYEE OBLIGATIONS
  1. Employees shall adhere to the following rules while working remotely.
  2. Employee must establish an appropriate work environment. Company is not responsible for setup costs.
  3. Employees must maintain productivity and performance.
  4. Employees must not use Company confidential information outside unless authorized.
  5. Employees must maintain proper internet connection and safe workspace.
  6. Company may require Employee to return to office at any time.
EQUIPMENT MANAGEMENT
  1. Company will determine the equipment required (hardware, software, modems, phone, office equipment).
  2. Equipment provided remains property of the Company. Employee must maintain and return it.
  3. Employee must protect Company equipment and data from theft or damage.
PERFORMANCE REVIEWS AND REPORTING
  1. Employees will report to Managers daily by submitting a daily report via the Company Portal.
  2. Employees may also be asked to report directly to the CEO via Video/Audio conference.
CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION AND DATA SECURITY
  1. Remote workers must protect Company information as per existing confidentiality agreements.
  2. Employees must comply with all agreements and policies including the confidentiality agreement signed upon employment.
  3. Employees must use secure VPN and avoid public/unsecured Wi-Fi.
ACKNOWLEDGEMENT
  1. I acknowledge I have reviewed the above policies and understand my responsibilities.
  2. I agree to report any actual or potential violations immediately.
  3. I agree to abide by these policies and understand failure may result in disciplinary action up to dismissal.
